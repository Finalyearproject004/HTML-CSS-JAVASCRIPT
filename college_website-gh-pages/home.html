Our Institutes

Sobhasaria Group of Institutions is one of the best technical institutes of Rajasthan, an Educational Venture of Rupa & Co. Kolkata emerged as an oasis among the dunes of Rajasthan. Sobhasaria Group of Institutions is one of the best technical institutes of Rajasthan where students of different states come to shape their careers in technology and management. It has state-of-the-art infrastructure with four-storied fully air-conditioned Central Library and Central Computer Centre, 37 acres lush green Wi-Fi enabled the campus to let its students live in an education conducive atmosphere that keeps them the most comfortable and enthusiastic while learning and provides them a glimpse of glorious life they can win for themselves. The experienced faculty members at Sobhasaria, with their industrial, academic and research skills inspire the students to move ahead in life with a winning attitude and make them industrial ready and expert at their workplace.
